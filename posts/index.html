Habits

There was an Old Person whose habits, Induced him to feed upon rabbits; When he'd eaten eighteen, He turned perfectly green, Upon which he relinquished those habits.

The Flapper

A nifty young flapper named Jane While walking was caught in the rain. She ran--almost flew, Her complexion did too, And she reached home exceedingly plain.
